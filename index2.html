Who We Are

Flemtech Africa —
Built for Renewable Energy

Flemtech Nigeria Ltd. specialises in Renewable Energy — Solar and Inverter Backup Systems, Small Hydro Power, Biomass, Waste-to-Energy plants, Land-Fill Gas Plants, and Wind Energy.

As Nigeria continues to pursue steady, uninterrupted power, we go beyond gas plants and invest in alternative technologies — especially for the rural 60% who need it most.

Our Vision

To be an Integrated Renewable Energy company and Independent Power Producer — delivering electric power from Renewable Energy Technologies (RETs) for the benefit of rural and urban communities across Africa.
